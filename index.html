Our Services

At Scot Bros Garage, we are committed to providing top-notch auto repair and maintenance services to keep your vehicle running smoothly. Our team of experienced technicians is dedicated to delivering quality work and exceptional customer service.

Vehicle Diagnostics

We use advanced diagnostic tools to identify and fix issues with your vehicle. Whether it's a check engine light or a mysterious noise, we can diagnose and repair it.

Mechanical Repairs

Our skilled technicians can handle all types of mechanical repairs, from engine and transmission repairs to brake and suspension work. We're here to get your vehicle back on the road safely.

Oil Change & Maintenance

Regular oil changes and maintenance services are essential for the longevity of your vehicle. We offer quick and reliable oil change services along with comprehensive maintenance checks to keep your car in optimal condition.

About Us

Scot Bros Garage has been a trusted name in the automotive industry for over four decades. Established in 1983, our family-owned business has proudly served the Glasgow community and beyond for 40 years.

Throughout our journey, we have built a reputation for excellence in auto repair and maintenance. Our commitment to quality workmanship, honest service, and customer satisfaction has remained unwavering since day one.

As a testament to our dedication, we have continually invested in state-of-the-art technology and training for our team of experts. This ensures that we can provide you with the best solutions for your vehicle, no matter its make or model.

At Scot Bros Garage, we look forward to serving you for many more years to come. Thank you for choosing us as your trusted partner for all your automotive needs.
